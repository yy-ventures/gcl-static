Who we are

We are a diverse, global, committed and passionate team with the mission, inspired by our Co-Founder and Chairman Nobel Peace Laureate Muhammad Yunus, of creating an ecosystem where social business strives to solve the most pressing social and environmental problems of our times.

learn more

What we do

We promote the concept of social business and inspire our ecosystem to change the world through our events, communication campaigns, and workshops for various organizations. We design and deliver:

  1. International platforms such as Global Social Business Summit, the flagship and biggest event on social business
  2. Transformative workshops and trainings
  3. Innovative campaigns around change and impact with social business

Educate

We design and offer a range of trainings and capacity building programs. Our goal is to transform mindsets and create responsible leaders who are aware of the role of social business in solving global challenges.

  1. Skill development programs such as Social Business Labs, ideation contests and system thinking workshops
  2. Leadership and capacity building programs to foster employee engagement and intrepreneurship programs with social business
  3. Strategic and self development programs to encourage responsible leadership at junior and senior level

We incubate social businesses by consulting corporations, foundations and social business entrepreneurs.

  1. Partner to form ambitious joint venture social businesses between corporates and the Grameen ecosystem
  2. Collaborate with public private and multi stakeholder partners to create an infrastructure for social businesses (e.g. Social Business City Concept)
  3. Shape an ecosystem for social businesses within a country or region through acceleration and incubation programs
